How long should I own a phone?

The why ?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer for how long to hold onto your phone, but here's a breakdown of factors to consider:
  • Functionality: Is your phone still meeting your needs? Does it run slow, have a cracked screen, or lack storage space? If so, it might be time for an upgrade.

  • Software Updates: Security updates are crucial to protect your phone from viruses and malware. Many manufacturers stop providing updates after 2-3 years for older models.

  • Battery Life: Does your phone die quickly and need frequent charging? Batteries usually degrade over time.
